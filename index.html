Big Bash League 2024-25

The 2024-25 Big Bash League (BBL) season, the 14th edition of Australia's premier T20 cricket competition, began on December 15, 2024, and will conclude with the final on January 27, 2024. Featuring eight city-based teams, the league has showcased thrilling perfomances, with the Hobart Hurricanes currently leading the standings. Defending champions Brisbane Heat have struggled, sitting fifth on the table. A major highlight is David Warner playing the full season for the Sydney Thunder, boosting the league's star power. Exciting matches, like the Melbourne Renegades' stunning chase against Perth Scorchers, have made this season a treat for fans.



South Africa's premier Twenty20 (SA20), 2025

SA20, officially known as the Betway SA20 for sponsorship reasons, is South Africa's premier Twenty20 (T20) cricket league, established in 2022 by Cricket South Africa (CSA) in partnership with SuperSport. The inaugural season took place in January 2023, featuring six city-based franchises: MI Cape Town, Durban's Super Giants, Johannesburg Super Kings, Paarl Royals, Pretoria Capitals, and Sunrisers Eastern Cape. These teams are owned by entties that also own successful Indian Premier League (IPL) franchises.

The league follows a round-robin format, with each team playing home and away matches, culminating in playoffs to determine the champion. The Sunrisers Eastern Cape clinched the title in both the inaugural and second seasons, making them the most successful team in the league's short history.

The league has significantly contributed to the development of cricket in South Africa, providing a platform or emerging players to showcase their skills alongside seasoned international stars. With substantial prize money and growing fan engagement, SA20 aims to replicate the success of other global T20 leagues, positioning itself as a key player in the international cricket calendar.



International League T20, 2025

The International League T20 (ILT20) 2025, officially known as the DP World ILT20, is the United Arab Emirates' premier Twenty20 cricket tournament. The second edition of the league is scheduled from January 11 to February 9, 2025m featuring six franchises: Dubai Capitals, MI Emirates, Abu Dhabi, Knight Riders, Desert Vipers, Gulf Giants, and Sharjah Warriors. Matches are being held accross venues in Dubai, Abu dhabi, and Sharjah. The tournament commenced with the Dubai Capitals narrowly defeating MI Emirates by 1 run in the opening match. As of January 14, 2025, several matches have been completed, with teams vying for top positions in the league standings. The ILT20 aims to enhance the cricketing landscape in the UAE by attracting international talent and providing a competitive platform for players worldwide.
